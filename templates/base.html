VISIT US

All the fun of the farm. We're moer than just a shop. Visit us for a great family day out. Discover what's involved in runnnig a farm and meet the animals. Addmission is refunded if you spend over £30 in our farm shop. Tickets can be purchased on arrival.

SHOP ONLINE

Unlike traditional farm shops you can now purchase any of our produce online. We deliver nationally for a small fee and locally for free. Be confident in your purchases as we offer a quality garauntee. If for any reason you are not satisfied with your purchases we will refund you.

HOME GROWN

All of our produce is home grown and organic. We do not use any commercial fertilisers at Oakfield farm as the environmental effects of using such chemicals are well document. We pride ourselves on being ethically and environmentally minded.
