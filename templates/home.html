Welcome to Oakfield Farm & Shop

We're a family run business with years of experience of producing the finest produce which we sell in our shop both on site and online. We are much more than your ordinary farm shop and offer fun days out for all the family. Come and visit us in person, meet the animals and enjoy all the fun at the farm. We often have additional events and run various festivals throughout the summer. Admission to the farm is just £2 per child and £3 per adult. Spend over £40 in our shop and admission is refunded. So what are you waiting for!

Explore the Grounds

Experience our beautiful farm and 80 acres of woodland. Experience nature, breathe in the fresh and relax whilst making precious memories with your friends and family.

Cwtch Corner

We love teaching little ones about how to care for animals, almost as much as they love to cuddle them. Come and visit our pets in cwtch corner. Your kids will love it.

A Working Farm

There are quite the variety of animals that help towards an authentic working farm, and we have them all! The newborn lambs are a particular delight at this time of year.

Home Grown

All of our produce is of the highest quality. This is something we take great pride in. Our animals are raised with love and all of our fruit and veg is 100% organic.

Meet the Animals

Spring is an especially exciting time here at Oakfield farm. This is your chance to meet the animals and get involved. In addition to our rabbits and guinea pigs, we have newborn lambs to bottle feed. We have so many animals here on the farm it's an ideal opportunity to learn about a variety of different breads. We give daily talks so be sure to check out our time table on arrival.

Farmers for Generations

Oakfield farm has been in our family for generations. Everyday on the farm is a joy for us and no two days are the same. We love tending to our animals and producing the highest quality produce any farm shop has to offer. Our love of farming has led to us opening our farm to anyone who wants to visit.
